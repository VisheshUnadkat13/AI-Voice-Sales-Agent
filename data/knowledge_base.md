## Company Overview
BrightWave CRM Solutions builds cloud-based CRM software for small and
mid-sized businesses in retail, manufacturing, and services. Founded 2019,
based in Ahmedabad, serving 400+ businesses across India.

## Product & Pricing
- **Starter Plan** — ₹999/month per user. Contact management, basic pipeline,
  email integration. Best for teams of 1-5.
- **Growth Plan** — ₹2,499/month per user. Adds automation, WhatsApp
  integration, custom reports. Best for teams of 5-20.
- **Enterprise Plan** — Custom pricing. Adds dedicated support, API access,
  custom workflows, on-premise option.
- All plans include a 14-day free trial, no credit card required.
- Annual billing gets a 20% discount vs monthly.

## Common Objections & How to Respond
- **"It's too expensive"** — Point to the ROI: customers typically recover the
  cost within 2 months from reduced lead leakage and faster follow-ups.
  Mention the annual discount and free trial to reduce risk.
- **"We already use [Competitor/Excel]"** — Acknowledge it's working for them
  today, ask what's manually painful about it, position BrightWave as
  removing that manual work rather than replacing something that's broken.
- **"I need to check with my team/partner"** — Respect this, offer to send a
  one-pager summary, propose a specific follow-up date rather than leaving it
  open-ended.
- **"Not interested right now"** — Ask if timing is the issue or the product
  isn't a fit; if timing, offer to follow up in 1-3 months; if not a fit,
  thank them and mark accordingly, don't push.

## Qualification Criteria (used to mark a lead "Qualified")
A lead is Qualified if ALL of these are true:
1. They currently handle sales/leads manually or with a tool they're unhappy with.
2. They have a team of 2+ people who would use the CRM.
3. They show some buying timeline (this quarter / next quarter) OR strong pain.
4. They are a decision-maker or can bring it to one quickly.
